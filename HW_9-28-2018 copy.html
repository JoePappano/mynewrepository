Here's how to make the best sandwich on earth --- PB&J

  1. Get 1 knife, 2 slices of bread, a jar of peanut butter, a plate and a jar of jelly.
  2. Put the plate in front of you.
  3. Put the slices of bread next to each other.
  4. Open up the jar of peanut butter and with the knife, scoop peanut butter onto the knife.
  5. Spread the peanut butter onto the chosen slice of bread.
  6. Open the jar of jelly and with the same knife, scoop jelly onto the knife.
  7. Spread the jelly onto the other slice of bread.
  8. When satisfied with amount of peanut butter and jelly on respective slices of bread, put the knife down.
  9. Then with both hands, pick up each slice of bread and put the sides of the slices that have been spread on together.
  10. Take your first bite and enjoy.
